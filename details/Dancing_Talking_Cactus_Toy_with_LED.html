Dancing Talking Cactus Mimicking Toy with Audio Recording and LED Light

Pbooo brings playful learning to life with this interactive cactus toy designed for babies and kids. Combining sound, motion, and LED effects, it's the perfect companion for playtime, bedtime, and every joyful moment in between.

Adjustable Volume for Peaceful Play

This cactus toy features a volume adjustment function with 5 levels, allowing you to customize the sound intensity. Whether you're playing with your baby or relaxing with a soft background melody, you can easily control the volume to suit your needs. Simply press and hold the right button for 5 seconds to increase or decrease the volume.

Interactive and Fun for All Ages

With its unique talking and dancing design, the Pbooo cactus toy is more than just a decorative item. It's an engaging tool for home decoration, baby play, and even educational settings. This cactus mimic is sure to bring laughter, joy, and a playful touch to any space.

Record and Repeat Your Voice with a Cactus Toy

Immerse your little one in personalized play with this talking cactus toy that allows you to record your own voice. It's also equipped with 120 pre-loaded songs for endless entertainment. Kids will love the fun of hearing their own voice repeated, while parents can create a memorable and interactive play experience.

Colorful LED Light for a Magical Touch

Enjoy a glowing atmosphere with the LED light effects of this cactus toy. It can double as a soft bedside lamp, illuminating your room with a warm and cheerful glow. Insert 3 AA batteries, power on, and press the button to start the cactus body movement and singing of joyful tunes.

Safe and Durable Material

Made from non-toxic and soft plush fabric, this cactus toy is designed for safe and comfortable play. It meets international safety standards, making it a trusted choice for young children. Clean it easily with a damp cloth and replace the batteries promptly when the volume or dancing becomes weak.

Simple and Easy to Operate

Follow these simple steps to get started with the cactus baby toy:

  • Step 1: Install 3 AA batteries at the bottom and tighten the screws.
  • Step 2: Press the black button to turn it on.
  • Step 3: Press the music button on the right to switch songs (120 songs available).
  • Step 4: Long press the left button to record your voice (up to 15 seconds). A beep will confirm the recording is complete.
  • Step 5: When turned on, speak within 50cm for the cactus to repeat your words.

The perfect blend of fun and function, this talking cactus toy is an ideal gift for babies, kids, and anyone who loves to play with joy.
