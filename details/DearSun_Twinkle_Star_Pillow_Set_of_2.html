DearSun 2pcs Star Colorful Night Light Plush Pillows
Twinkle Star Night Light Plush Pillows

Elevate your bedtime with our 2-piece set of creative star-shaped plush pillows. Made from 100% polyester and filled with premium PP cotton, these pillows are designed for comfort and durability. Each piece is 13.8 inches in size, offering a perfect fit for snuggling and resting.

Choose from a wide range of 10 enchanting colors including Yellow, Pink, Blue, Green, Orange, Watermelon Red, Deep Pink, and Light bluish green. These pillows are not only stylish but also soft and huggable, making them ideal for bedtime or bedtime decorations.

Safety is a top priority with our plush pillows. The eyes, blush, and mouth are intricately embroidered, ensuring no small parts that could pose a choking hazard. The included hanging rope allows for easy display and adds an extra layer of convenience.

Each pillow features a built-in switch button that turns the light on and off. When activated, it cycles through a spectrum of stylish colors, creating a magical and calming ambiance. Please note that these pillows should not be submerged in water due to the integrated LED lighting. Powered by 2 AA batteries (not included), suitable for children 36 months and above.

Whether it's for a birthday, Christmas, Valentine's, or just a thoughtful gift, our star pillows are a perfect way to add comfort and joy to any occasion. They blend functionality with style, serving as a cozy backrest, a huggable plush toy, and a charming decorative piece for your home.
