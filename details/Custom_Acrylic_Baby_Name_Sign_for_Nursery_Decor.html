Custom Baby Name Sign | Personalized Nursery Wall Decor | Acrylic Letters for Kids Room

Add a personal, meaningful touch to your baby's nursery with our custom name sign. Handmade in the USA, this acrylic name plaque is perfect for hanging above your child's bed or dresser, and can also serve as a beautiful decor piece in their room or at a baby shower.

Durable & Elegant Material

Constructed from 1/8" (3mm) thick, high-quality acrylic, our name signs are built to last and maintain their shape over time. The smooth, sturdy surface is ideal for both decorative and functional use, making it a great addition to any baby room.

Choose Your Style & Color

Customize your sign with one of our over 40 color options, including Mirror Gold, Silver, Rose Gold, Glitter Pink, Purple, Blue, Clear, White, Black, Red, Green, Yellow, and Marble finishes. Keep in mind that colors may vary slightly on screen due to lighting and display settings.
